<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Optical liquid level switches are categorized into two types: digital and analog. Digital optical liquid level switches use digital signals to detect liquids. They are equipped with sensors that emit light and measure its reflection to determine the presence or absence of liquid. This type offers high accuracy and reliability. On the other hand, analog optical liquid level switches utilize analog signals for liquid detection. They operate based on the principles of light reflection and refraction, providing continuous outputs proportional to the liquid level.</p></p>
