<p><p>The Thermoelectric Cooler Module (TEM) market is categorized into two types: Single Stage Module and Multistage Module. Single stage modules consist of a single pair of Peltier elements, which allow heat transfer in a single direction. They are commonly used for smaller cooling applications. Conversely, multistage modules are composed of multiple pairs of Peltier elements, enabling heat transfer in multiple directions. This makes them suitable for larger and more complex cooling systems. Both types cater to different cooling requirements and applications within various industries.</p></p>
