<p><p>The Miniature Torque Sensors market is segmented into two main types: Static Torque Sensors and Dynamic Torque Sensors. Static Torque Sensors are designed to measure torque in a stationary or fixed position, providing accurate readings of torque applied to a rotating shaft. On the other hand, Dynamic Torque Sensors are specifically designed to measure torque in applications that involve rotational or dynamic motion, providing real-time measurements of torque changes during operation. These sensors play a crucial role in various industries, enabling better performance monitoring, control, and optimization of torque-based processes.</p></p>
