<p><p>Expanded Polytetrafluoroethylene (ePTFE) Microporous Membranes are widely used in various industries due to their exceptional chemical resistance, thermal stability, and excellent permeability properties. They are available in two market types: Micron and Submicron. Micron membranes are designed with larger pores ranging from 1 to 100 micrometers, making them suitable for applications requiring high flow rates. On the other hand, Submicron membranes have smaller pores less than 1 micrometer in size and are crucial for applications requiring fine filtration, such as gas purification and water treatment.</p></p>
