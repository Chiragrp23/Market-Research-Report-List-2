<p><p>One-part epoxy resin adhesives come in two main market types: heat curing and ultraviolet (UV) curing. Heat curing adhesives require the application of heat for the adhesive to cure and achieve its full strength. This process is usually achieved by placing the bonded components in an oven or using a heat gun. On the other hand, UV curing adhesives cure when exposed to ultraviolet light. This type of adhesive is typically used in applications where fast curing and high bond strength are desired.</p></p>
