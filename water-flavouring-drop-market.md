<p><p>The water flavouring drop market can be categorized into four types: sweetened, stevia-sweetened, artificially sweetened, and unsweetened. Sweetened flavouring drops typically contain sugar or high-fructose corn syrup to add a sweet taste to water. Stevia-sweetened drops use the natural, zero-calorie sweetener stevia to provide a sweet flavor without the added calories. Artificially sweetened drops are sugar-free and instead use artificial sweeteners like aspartame or sucralose. Unsweetened drops, as the name suggests, do not contain any added sweeteners and provide only the flavor without altering the sweetness of the water.</p></p>
