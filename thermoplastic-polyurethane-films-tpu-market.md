The TPU market consists of different types, including Polyester TPU, Polyether TPU, and Polycaprolactone TPU. Polyester TPU is known for its high tensile strength and excellent weather resistance. Polyether TPU is well-regarded for its outstanding hydrolytic stability and microbial resistance. Polycaprolactone TPU, on the other hand, offers exceptional flexibility and low-temperature performance. These different types of TPUs cater to specific applications and requirements but share the common characteristics of TPU films.</p></p>
