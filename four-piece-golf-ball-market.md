<p><p>The four-piece golf ball market consists of two main types: the Surlyn golf ball and the urethane golf ball. Surlyn golf balls are known for their durability and distance. They have a harder cover made of ionomer resin, which allows for greater roll and lower spin. On the other hand, urethane golf balls have a softer cover made of polyurethane, providing enhanced control and feel around the greens. Urethane balls are often the preferred choice for professional golfers and players seeking superior performance in terms of spin and control.</p></p>
