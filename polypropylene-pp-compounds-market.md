<p><p>Polypropylene (PP) compounds are versatile materials widely used in various industries. Homopolymer PP compounds consist of a single type of monomer, resulting in a high melting point and excellent stiffness. They offer good chemical resistance and are suitable for applications where strength is crucial. Copolymer PP compounds, on the other hand, are made up of two different types of monomers, resulting in improved impact resistance and flexibility. They are commonly used in applications requiring toughness, such as automotive parts, packaging, and pipe systems.</p></p>
