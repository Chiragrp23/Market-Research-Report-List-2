<p><p>The Chip-on-board (CoB) LED market comprises various types: lateral chip, vertical chip, and flip chip. Lateral chip CoB LEDs have the chip mounted sideways, which allows for better heat dissipation and wider light-emitting surface area. Vertical chip CoB LEDs have the chip mounted vertically, enabling higher light extraction efficiency and better color mixing capabilities. Flip chip CoB LEDs feature an inverted chip structure, which improves light extraction and thermal management. These various types of CoB LEDs offer different advantages and applications in the market.</p></p>
