<p><p>Low voltage switchboards are electrical distribution boards that house low voltage electrical components. There are two types: single front and double front. A single front switchboard has all its functional units facing one side, making it suitable for installation against a wall. On the other hand, a double front switchboard has functional units facing both the front and rear, allowing for installation in open spaces or against a wall with access from both sides. These two types offer flexibility in terms of installation and accessibility, catering to different space and operational requirements.</p></p>
