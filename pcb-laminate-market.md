<p><p>The PCB laminate market consists of several types, including glass fabric, epoxy resin, kraft paper, and phenolic resin. Glass fabric laminates are made by impregnating glass fibers with epoxy resin, providing high mechanical strength and electrical insulation properties. Epoxy resin laminates offer excellent dimensional stability, heat resistance, and a smooth surface. Kraft paper laminates use layers of cellulose material impregnated with phenolic resin, ensuring good insulation and easy machining. Lastly, phenolic resin laminates are made from layers of paper impregnated with phenolic resin, offering high mechanical strength and electrical insulation.</p></p>
