<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The roofing underlayment market consists of three types: non-bitumen synthetic, asphalt-saturated felt, and rubberized asphalt. Non-bitumen synthetic underlayment is made of polypropylene or polyester and offers excellent resistance to tears and punctures. Asphalt-saturated felt is a traditional choice, comprising organic or cellulose fibers coated with asphalt. It provides good protection against water penetration and acts as a moisture barrier. Rubberized asphalt underlayment is a blend of asphalt and synthetic rubber, providing enhanced durability and flexibility. It is commonly used in areas prone to severe weather conditions.</p></p>
